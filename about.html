Beyond Code

I'm passionate about creating technology that serves everyone. I believe the best digital experiences are inclusive by design—accessible, joyful, and human-centered. Whether it's ensuring keyboard navigation works flawlessly, implementing proper ARIA labels, or adding delightful micro-interactions, I'm driven by the conviction that thoughtful design and inclusive development make technology better for all users.

I'm particularly energized by projects at the intersection of creativity and functionality. I love exploring how design systems can be both rigorous and playful, how security can be user-friendly, and how the web can be a more welcoming space for diverse communities. My commitment to accessibility isn't just about compliance—it's about building a more equitable digital world where everyone can participate fully.

When I'm not coding, you'll find me deep in a good video game (currently obsessed with narrative-driven indie titles), hunting for rare records to add to my collection, or experimenting with music production. I'm fascinated by how game design teaches us about user experience, how vinyl's tactile nature reminds us that technology should be tangible and meaningful, and how creating music parallels the iterative process of building software. These hobbies keep me curious, creative, and constantly thinking about how we interact with the systems around us—digital and analog alike.
